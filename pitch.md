# PoliSwipe -- Pitch

## One-liner

PoliSwipe turns civic engagement into a swipe, swipe right on a local bill, Claude tells you more and draft responses.

## Elevator (30 sec)

Young people aren't apathetic about politics, they're stuck on step one. PoliSwipe is a Tinder-style deck of real local bills, rallies, and campus issues -- swipe right and Claude drafts a personalized email to your actual representative; swipe down and ask Claude anything about the policy in plain English. We collapsed twelve steps of civic friction into a single thumb gesture.

## Tagline variants

- Democracy, one swipe at a time.
- Swipe right. Email sent.
- From "I care" to "I did something" in 15 seconds.
- Civic engagement that feels like a feed, not a form.
